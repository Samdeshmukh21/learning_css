Marine Drive, also known as the Queen's Necklace, is a 3.6 kilometer long, C-shaped boulevard in South Mumbai that stretches along the Arabian Sea. It's a popular spot for locals and tourists alike, offering stunning sunset views and a vibrant atmosphere. The promenade is known for its scenic beauty, architectural charm, and the way the streetlights resemble a necklace when illuminated at night.
